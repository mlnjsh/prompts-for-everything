## Cinematic & Film

### 1. Cinematic Landscape
```
A sweeping aerial shot over a vast mountain range at golden hour. Camera slowly
pans from left to right revealing a crystal-clear lake below reflecting the orange
and purple sky. Volumetric light rays pierce through scattered clouds. Shot on
ARRI Alexa, cinematic color grading, 4K, shallow depth of field.
```

### 2. Dramatic Character Shot
```
Close-up portrait of an elderly man sitting in a dimly lit room. Single warm light
source from a window to the left. He slowly turns to face the camera with a
contemplative expression. Dust particles floating in the light beam. Film grain,
anamorphic lens flare, Kodak film look.
```

### 3. Sci-Fi City
```
A sprawling futuristic city at night seen from a flying vehicle perspective.
Neon lights reflecting off wet streets below. Holographic advertisements float
between buildings. Flying cars streak by leaving light trails. Camera pushes
forward through the cityscape. Blade Runner aesthetic, cyberpunk, volumetric fog.
```

### 4. Time-Lapse Nature
```
Time-lapse of a flower blooming in a forest clearing. Sun moves across the sky
from dawn to dusk casting shifting shadows. The flower opens petal by petal
revealing vibrant colors. Macro lens, shallow depth of field, smooth motion,
nature documentary style.
```

### 5. Action Sequence
```
A person running through a narrow alley in the rain. Camera follows from behind
in a steady tracking shot. Puddles splash with each step, neon signs reflect off
the wet ground. The person vaults over a fence and continues running. Slow motion
at the jump, then back to normal speed. Gritty urban cinematography.
```

## Product & Commercial

### 6. Product Showcase
```
A sleek smartphone rotates slowly on a reflective black surface. Soft studio
lighting with subtle highlights on the edges. The screen lights up displaying
a colorful interface. Camera orbits 360 degrees. Clean minimal background,
product photography lighting, commercial quality.
```

### 7. Food Commercial
```
Slow motion pour of golden honey over a stack of fresh pancakes. Steam rises from
the warm pancakes. Camera at a slight angle, macro lens capturing every detail
of the honey's viscosity. Bright, appetizing lighting. Professional food
photography style, 120fps slow motion.
```

### 8. Luxury Brand
```
A woman walks through a grand marble hallway in an elegant evening dress. Camera
dollies backward leading her through the space. Chandeliers sparkle overhead.
Each step echoes. Soft backlight creates a silhouette effect. Fashion film
aesthetic, Vogue editorial style.
```

## Animation & Motion Graphics

### 9. Abstract Fluid Art
```
Colorful ink drops falling into water in slow motion. Deep blue, magenta, and gold
inks swirling and mixing creating abstract patterns. Camera captures from above
looking down. Macro lens, high speed photography, black background.
```

### 10. Geometric Animation
```
Abstract geometric shapes — cubes, spheres, and triangles — floating in a void.
They slowly assemble into a recognizable pattern (a human face silhouette).
Smooth motion, satisfying transitions. Metallic gold and matte black materials.
Isometric camera angle, motion graphics style.
```

### 11. Logo Reveal
```
Particles of light converge from the edges of the frame toward the center.
They swirl in a vortex pattern and coalesce to form a [describe your logo].
A shockwave of light emanates outward as the logo solidifies.
Dark background, cinematic particle effects, 3D rendered quality.
```

## Nature & Wildlife

### 12. Underwater World
```
Camera slowly descends into a vibrant coral reef. Schools of tropical fish swim
past the lens. Sunlight filters through the water creating caustic light patterns
on the coral. A sea turtle glides gracefully through the frame. Deep blue water,
documentary style, natural lighting.
```

### 13. Storm Timelapse
```
Dramatic time-lapse of storm clouds forming over a prairie. Lightning strikes
in the distance. The clouds roll and twist in supernatural patterns. Camera is
stationary on a tripod. Golden wheat field in the foreground. National Geographic
style, wide angle lens.
```

### 14. Aurora Borealis
```
Northern lights dancing over a frozen lake in Iceland. Green and purple curtains
of light ripple across the starry sky. Perfect reflection in the still water below.
A lone cabin with warm light in the windows at the edge of the lake.
Long exposure photography style, wide angle.
```

## Educational & Explainer

### 15. Solar System Tour
```
Camera flies past each planet in the solar system starting from the Sun.
Smooth transition from Mercury through Venus, Earth (with visible blue oceans
and clouds), Mars, Jupiter (with Great Red Spot), Saturn (rings detail), Uranus,
Neptune. Scale somewhat compressed for visual effect. Space documentary style.
```

### 16. Cell Division
```
Microscopic view of a cell undergoing mitosis. The chromosomes line up,
duplicate, and pull apart. The cell membrane pinches in the middle and splits
into two daughter cells. Scientific visualization style, soft blue-white
lighting, electron microscope aesthetic.
```

## Mood & Atmosphere

### 17. Cozy Rainy Day
```
Interior of a cozy coffee shop on a rainy day. View through a foggy window
with rain droplets streaming down. Warm amber lighting inside. A cup of coffee
steams on a wooden table. Blurred city lights and people with umbrellas outside.
Nostalgic, warm tones, shallow depth of field.
```

### 18. Ethereal Dream
```
A person floating weightlessly in a vast field of glowing dandelion seeds.
The seeds drift slowly upward like stars. Soft dreamy lighting, pastel colors
— lavender, soft pink, mint green. The person is backlit creating a halo effect.
Surreal, dreamlike, ethereal atmosphere.
```

### 19. Vintage Memory
```
A family having a picnic in a sun-dappled meadow. Shot in Super 8 film style
with visible grain, light leaks, and slightly washed-out colors. Warm vintage
color palette. Camera gently sways as if handheld. Nostalgic summer feeling,
1970s aesthetic.
```

## Advanced Prompt Modifiers

### Camera Movements
- `"Dolly zoom"` — Vertigo/Hitchcock effect
- `"Crane shot"` — Rising above the scene
- `"Steadicam tracking"` — Smooth follow shot
- `"Whip pan"` — Fast horizontal movement
- `"Drone shot ascending"` — Rising aerial
- `"Push in"` — Camera moves toward subject
- `"Pull out reveal"` — Camera backs away to reveal context

### Lighting Keywords
- `"Golden hour"` — Warm sunset light
- `"Blue hour"` — Cool twilight
- `"Rembrandt lighting"` — Dramatic single-source
- `"High-key"` — Bright, even, minimal shadows
- `"Low-key"` — Dark, dramatic, high contrast
- `"Practical lighting"` — In-scene light sources only
- `"Volumetric"` — Visible light beams in atmosphere

### Style References
- `"Shot on ARRI Alexa"` — Cinematic film look
- `"35mm film"` — Classic cinema grain
- `"Anamorphic lens"` — Wide format with flares
- `"Kodachrome"` — Vintage warm film stock
- `"Teal and orange grade"` — Hollywood blockbuster look
- `"Wes Anderson style"` — Symmetry, pastels, whimsy
- `"Christopher Nolan"` — IMAX, practical effects, scale

### Resolution & Quality
- `"4K, high detail"` — Maximum quality
- `"120fps slow motion"` — Ultra smooth
- `"8mm film"` — Lo-fi vintage
- `"HDR, wide color gamut"` — Vibrant colors
- `"Film grain, 24fps"` — Cinematic standard
